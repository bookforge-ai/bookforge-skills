# Traction — Agent Skills

13 agent skills for startup founders and growth marketers, distilled from
*Traction: A Startup Guide to Getting Customers* by Gabriel Weinberg and Justin Mares
(Portfolio, 2014). The skills cover the full growth stack: Bullseye channel selection,
phase-appropriate strategy, cheap channel testing, critical path planning, and 9 deep-dive
acquisition channels (viral loops, PR, SEM, SEO, content+email, engineering-as-marketing,
sales, business development, and existing platform leverage). Each skill encodes the
reasoning — not just the steps — so your agent can handle edge cases, not just textbook scenarios.

## The 13 Skills

### Foundation (Level 0)

1. **bullseye-channel-selection** — The entry point. Systematic 5-step channel selection: brainstorm all 19 channels, rank into Inner Circle / Potential / Long-shot, pick exactly 3, test in parallel, focus on the winner.
2. **startup-traction-strategy-by-phase** — Diagnose startup phase (I: making → II: marketing → III: scaling), apply the 50% Rule, match channels to phase, detect the Product Trap.
3. **traction-channel-testing** — Cheap validation tests using the 4-question protocol (CAC, volume, quality, time-to-acquire) and CAC/LTV spreadsheet. Law of Shitty Click-Throughs detection.
4. **startup-critical-path-planning** — Set one quantified traction goal, enumerate necessary milestones, apply the binary on-path/off-path filter, cascade to teams.

### Channel Skills (Level 1)

5. **viral-growth-loop-design** — K = i × conversion%, 7 viral loop types, cycle time optimization, 4 viral mistakes check.
6. **startup-pr-outreach** — Media chain (small → top), HARO, two pitch templates, 6 PR mistakes check. Covers stunts and unconventional PR.
7. **sem-performance-optimization** — CTR/CPC/CPA formulas, Quality Score benchmarks (avg 2.0%, low 1.5%), profitability filter, keyword pruning.
8. **seo-channel-strategy** — Fat-head vs long-tail binary decision, 10% capture test, content production pipelines, black-hat avoidance.
9. **content-and-email-marketing** — 4-stage lifecycle (Acquisition → Activation → Retention → Revenue), behavioral triggers, CEO email pattern.
10. **engineering-as-marketing** — Free tool design, single-input pattern, HubSpot Marketing Grader reference, value-first-then-capture flow.
11. **startup-sales-process** — SPIN Selling, A/B/C lead tiering (66-75% on A deals), PNAME qualification, Technology Tourist and False Change Agent trap detection.
12. **business-development-pipeline** — 5 partnership types, attribute-based selection, warm introductions, 1-page term sheets, Low-touch BD 2.0.
13. **existing-platform-leverage** — Parasitic growth pattern, platform gap analysis, Day-1 launch strategy, dependency risk mitigation.
